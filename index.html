The Standard of Excellence

DNA Football is the premier elite girls' flag football organization in Northern New Jersey. We are dedicated to setting the standard of excellence in the fastest-growing sport in the world, providing a professional, comprehensive development pathway that empowers student-athletes to reach their highest potential.

Operating with the structure and rigor of the nation's top youth sports clubs, we provide a year-round environment designed for athletes committed to mastering their craft.

From foundational mechanics to high-level schematic execution on the travel circuit, DNA Football prepares players for the next level—whether that is dominating high school leagues, competing for state titles, or preparing for the collegiate stage.

The 12-Month Pipeline

Our organization utilizes a year-round developmental curriculum ensuring continuous athlete progression.

  • Fall: Foundational Play Local leagues focused on game IQ and situational awareness.
  • Winter: Technical Academies Specialized indoor clinics focused on advanced mechanical repetition.
  • Spring: Regional Showcases Pre-season tournament prep and local competitive circuits.
  • Summer: Elite Travel Circuit High-exposure national tournaments and championship events.

Our Club Pillars

What separates an elite club from recreational play is the quality of instruction, the level of competition, and an uncompromising commitment to athlete safety.

Championship Pedigree

Our staff doesn't just run drills; they build champions. DNA Football is anchored by professionals boasting decades of championship experience at both the high school and club levels, bringing collegiate-level curriculums to the youth game.

Certified & Vetted

We prioritize the safety and technical integrity of our athletes above all else. Every coach within our organization is fully background-checked, SafeSport compliant, and holds official USA Football Certification.

National Affiliations

To provide our athletes with premium exposure, we are dual-sanctioned by USA Football (the official Olympic scouting pipeline) and NFL FLAG, securing access to exclusive national championship circuits.
